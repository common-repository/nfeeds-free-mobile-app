== Description ==
[NFeeds](http://nfeeds.tooleap.com?ref=wpplugin) | **Free Mobile App** Android Native app

Whether you have a blog or a website, your most important asset is your loyal readers. [NFeeds](http://nfeeds.tooleap.com?ref=wpplugin) allows you to provide an app especially designed for your readers, so they can get instant access and get notified when you post new content. 

Install the plugin now, and immediately get a link to download the app, which you can share with your readers.

Feature :

* **100% Free** - No hidden fees
* **Beautiful design**
* **Unlimited Notification** - Your readers will get notified whenever you post new content
* **Instant Access** - The app is ready within seconds, no need to upload to the app store

== Installation ==

VIA THE WORDPRESS PLUGINS PANEL

1. Go to the Mobile App Generator link inside the WordPress settings menu.
2. Click on "Generate my app" and you'll immediately get a link to download your app.
3. All is done! Share this link with your followers via your wordpress site and social networks.
